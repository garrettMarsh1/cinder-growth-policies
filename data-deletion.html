Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: January 30, 2025

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data from Cindy by Cinder Growth, please follow these steps:

Email Request Method:

  1. Send an email to contact@cindergrowth.com
  2. Use the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include the following information:
    • Your full name
    • Email address associated with your account
    • Business name (if applicable)
    • A statement requesting data deletion

Platform Deauthorization:

To immediately disconnect platform integrations:

  1. Log into your Cindy account
  2. Navigate to Settings > Connected Accounts
  3. Click "Disconnect" next to each connected platform

Processing Time

We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email once completed.

Data Retention Notice

Please note that some data may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes. This may include:

  • Transaction records required for tax purposes
  • Data necessary for legal compliance
  • Anonymized analytics data

Contact Information

For questions about data deletion or to check the status of your request, contact us at:

  • Email: contact@cindergrowth.com
  • Website: https://cindergrowth.com
